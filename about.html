About Us

Mungadi Tech is a digital learning hub focused on empowering children with essential computer and digital skills. Founded with a vision to equip young minds with the tools needed to thrive in a technology-driven world, we provide hands-on training in various ICT disciplines.

Our mission is to nurture creativity, critical thinking, and digital fluency through beginner-friendly, engaging, and practical training sessions. We aim to bridge the digital gap by making computer education accessible and exciting for kids in our community.

We are proud to be a team of passionate educators, tech professionals, and youth mentors dedicated to raising the next generation of digital innovators.
